about app

This is an innovative random video calling app that allows users to enjoy and explore different cultures and languages around the world and make new friends.
WhoAreU? is an innovative random video calling app that allows users to enjoy and explore different cultures and languages around the world and make new friends and connections.
The app aims to break down language and cultural barriers and communicate with people from all over the world in real time.

Features:
Random video calls: "WhoAreU? users can enjoy random video calls with users from all over the world through "WhoAreU? It is a great opportunity to explore new friends and different cultures.
Multilingual Support: The app supports a variety of languages, breaking down language barriers and helping you communicate smoothly with other users. It is a great place to learn languages and hone new language skills.
No registration required: There is no complicated registration process to use the app. Simply download the app and enjoy easy communication anytime, anywhere.
Block function: It is easy to deal with unwanted people and inappropriate behavior in the app. You can block objectionable users and enjoy communicating with them in peace.
WhoAreU? is a platform to respect different cultures and make new friends and connections. Dive into new adventures, connect with people from all over the world, and explore different languages and cultures.
